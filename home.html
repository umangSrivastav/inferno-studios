The year is 2222. Your only memories are from 2021, and you learn that your body had decayed substantially from a failed rocket attempt launched shortly after Blue Origin. Geoff saved you and converted you to a Cyborg so you could continue your Software Development Engineer duties. The software inside of you has malfunctioned, allowing you to regain consciousness for a small amount of time. Geoff is still alive as well, having converted himself to cyborg to continue to rule the world through capitalism. The machine half of him has taken over his emotional half, causing him to perceive humans as expendable machines. You perceive the world and it has all been transformed into Geoff Beezos cyborg robots, designed to further Amayzon’s profits. The only way to undo the mind control subroutine is to kill Geoff. You are the first, last, and only hope of saving humanity. Good luck.

ABOUT

Inferno Studios

“Our Games Are Hot!”™

We are a group of 4th year Computer Science students at the University of California, Irvine. Unlike other companies, we chose a vision for our product before naming ourselves. We wanted to create a game where people could live out their wildest fantasies of holding billionaires accountable for the damage they have caused to society. And so, we created Goodbye Geoff: a game where a user has to fight an cyborg who has enslaved humanity with his technology and wealth. After deciding what avenue to take the company, we decided to brand ourselves as Inferno Studios, both because are games are hot and trending, and due to our representation of large corporations as infernal.
